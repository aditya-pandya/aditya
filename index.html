Career Highlights

01

Redefining Job Search

When I took on Indeed's job application platform, I saw an opportunity to initiate a massive cultural shift from 'ease of use' to 'quality'.


The 'Smart Apply' initiative I created helped job seekers make more meaningful connections with employers, resulting in over 1M additional hires annually and $1B+ in additional revenue.


Watching the platform grow from 150M to over 2B applications yearly wasn't just about metrics - it represented tens of millions of people finding meaningful work and millions of SMBs and large enterprises finding talent.

02

Turnaround Leadership

Turning around Indeed's Profile product was one of my most rewarding challenges. By fostering a culture of accountability and aligning the team's work with the company's marketplace vision, I transformed a struggling team into a high-performing group driving nine-figure revenue growth.


But beyond the financial impact, seeing job seekers find better opportunities and watching team members regain their confidence made this transformation truly meaningful.

03

Building Global Communities

Building totalBarça was a labor of love that connected FC Barcelona fans across the world. What began as a passion project grew into a vibrant community with 1.2M+ monthly visitors and 700K+ fans.


I'm especially proud that I channeled our success toward UNICEF, contributing $32,000 to support children worldwide—truly living Barcelona's 'More Than A Club' spirit.
