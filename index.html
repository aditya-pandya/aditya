Transformative Achievements

01

Reinventing Marketplace Dynamics

At Indeed, I led a fundamental shift that transformed the company's flagship job application platform from a volume-focused to a quality-centered marketplace, challenging a decade and a half of company orthodoxy around "ease above all else".
This vision required aligning 20+ cross-functional teams across six countries and reimagining the job seeking and hiring journey for 45M+ job seekers and 2M+ employers. The result was "Smart Apply," a tailored, guidance-driven job application process that drove a significant increase in hiring outcomes.
The impact was exceptional: 1M+ additional people hired annually, $1B+ in incremental revenue, and a complete shift in Indeed's product philosophy. By extending this platform to power job applications across Glassdoor, SimplyHired and partner sites, I established market leadership that created a sustainable competitive advantage in HR Tech.
02

Transforming Team Performance

My leadership approach consistently transforms team performance at every scale. I evolved Indeed Apply from a 5-person startup-like team into a 150+ person organization across six countries, implementing a "follow the sun" model that enabled continuous innovation and drove 13x product growth.
When I inherited Indeed's Profile team, the foundation of the company's billion-dollar sourcing business, it was on the verge of a collapse. I executed a comprehensive turnaround by restructuring into focused pods, rebuilding leadership capabilities, and establishing frameworks that fostered both accountability and innovation.
Within 18 months, this once-struggling team drove $400M in revenue growth and became central to Indeed's marketplace strategy. The true testament to my leadership approach is how well my teams perform after my departure, maintaining momentum and continuing to innovate independently.
03

Pioneering AI-Powered Experiences

I led Indeed's shift to AI-first user experiences, creating an intelligent content engine that collected 215M+ job seeker qualifications and preferences every month to improve matching for millions of job seekers and employers. This shift transformed Indeed from a search-first job board to a recommendations-driven job marketplace, significantly improving satisfaction on both sides.
By integrating machine learning throughout the job seeking journey, I reduced low-quality job applications by 13M+ every month. I also developed an AI-powered interview scheduling platform that reached 10M+ interviews annually within just 18 months of launch.
Additionally, I drove the first-ever strategic integration between Indeed and Glassdoor, navigating complex stakeholder dynamics to align two sister companies with different priorities and cultures. This partnership strengthened both companies' market positions against competitors.
