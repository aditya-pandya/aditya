Career Highlights

01

Marketplace Transformation at Scale

At Indeed, I orchestrated a strategic pivot that transformed the company's flagship job application platform from a volume-driven to a quality-focused marketplace, challenging a decade of company orthodoxy around "ease above all" to better serve both sides of the market.
This vision required aligning 20+ cross-functional teams across six countries and reimagining the user journey for 45M+ job seekers and 2M+ employers. The result was "Smart Apply," a tailored, guidance-driven application process that drove a 9.2% increase in hiring outcomes.
The impact was transformative: 1M+ additional people hired annually, $1B+ in incremental revenue, and a fundamental shift in Indeed's product development philosophy. By extending this platform to power applications on Glassdoor and partner sites, I established market leadership in HR tech and created a sustainable competitive advantage.
02

Building High-Performance Organizations

I've consistently built and scaled teams that deliver extraordinary results. I grew Indeed Apply from a 5-person team into a 150+ person cross-functional organization across six countries, instituting a "follow the sun" model that enabled 24/7 innovation and 13x product growth.
When Indeed's Profile team, the backbone of the company's billion-dollar sourcing business, was on the verge of collapse, I led a comprehensive turnaround. I rebuilt team confidence and capabilities by restructuring into focused pods, revamping leadership, and instituting processes that fostered accountability and innovation.
Within 18 months, this once-struggling team drove $400M in revenue growth and became central to Indeed's marketplace strategy. The true testament to my leadership approach is how well my teams perform after my departure, maintaining momentum and continuing to innovate independently.
03

Future-Focused Product Leadership

I pioneered Indeed's shift to AI-first user experiences, creating an intelligent content engine that analyzed 215M+ job seeker qualifications monthly to transform Indeed from a search-first job board to a recommendations-driven marketplace, significantly improving match quality.
By integrating machine learning throughout the user journey, I reduced unqualified applications by 13M+ monthly while implementing an AI-powered interview scheduling platform that reached 10M+ interviews annually within just 18 months of launch.
I also led the first strategic integration between Indeed and Glassdoor, navigating complex stakeholder dynamics to align two sister companies with different priorities. This success led to a broader partnership where Glassdoor ultimately exited the job search business entirely, strengthening both companies' market positions against competitors.
